The Legalese

Mission Statement

Fits Like A Glove is a social organization made up of individuals regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity/expression, or disability who enjoy wearing, admiring, using and celebrating gloves, leather and uniforms in many aspects of their lives. The organization exists to promote interaction between its members and will remain a social and fundraising group in its constitution.
